How does tipping work?

A tip is a voluntary amount added to your bill as a gesture of appreciation for good service. This calculator helps you split it fairly.

Select a tip percentage, enter your bill amount and number of people, and we'll handle the math:

  • Tip amount — what gets added
  • Total bill — original + tip
  • Per person — your share of the total

Note: Tipping customs vary by country and setting. In Nigeria, tipping is appreciated but not always expected — use your own judgment based on the service you received.
