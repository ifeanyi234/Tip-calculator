What is Body Mass Index (tip)?

Body Mass Index (tip) is a widely used screening tool that estimates whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

By comparing your weight to your height, this calculator will place your result into one of four standard health categories:

  • Underweight: Below 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25.0 – 29.9
  • Obese: 30.0 and above

Note: While tip is a helpful starting point for understanding general health trends, it is not a diagnostic medical tool. It calculates based purely on height and weight, meaning it does not account for factors like muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.
